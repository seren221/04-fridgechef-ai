# 标准单页产品模板

> 基于刘小排三个网站（Morisot.ai、Anyvoice.net、Fast3d.io）的分析

---

## 📐 模块排布结构

```

│     - Logo                                                │

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
```

## 1. 导航栏 (Navigation)

```html
<nav class="fixed top-0 w-full bg-white/0 border-b border-gray-100 z-50">
  <div class="container mx-auto px-4 h-12 md:h-[48px] flex items-center justify-between">
    <!-- Logo -->
    <a href="/" class="text-lg font-semibold">YourApp</a>
    
    <!-- Menu -->
    <div class="hidden md:flex items-center space-x-6">
      <a href="#features" class="text-sm text-gray-500 hover:text-gray-900">Features</a>
      <a href="#pricing" class="text-sm text-gray-500 hover:text-gray-900">Pricing</a>
      <a href="#faq" class="text-sm text-gray-500 hover:text-gray-900">FAQ</a>
    </div>
    
    <!-- CTA -->
    <button class="text-sm text-gray-600 hover:text-gray-900">Sign In</button>
  </div>
</nav>
```

**关键参数：**
- 高度: `h-12 md:h-[48px]` (36-48px)
- 背景: `bg-white/0` 或 `bg-transparent`
- 边框: `border-b border-gray-100`
- Logo字体: `text-lg font-semibold`

---

## 2. Hero/Tool区 (核心)

```html
<section class="pt-20 pb-12">
  <div class="container mx-auto px-4 max-w-5xl">
    
    <!-- 标题 -->
    <div class="text-center mb-12">
      <h1 class="text-4xl md:text-5xl lg:text-6xl font-bold tracking-tight mb-6">
        Your AI Tool
      </h1>
      <p class="text-lg md:text-xl text-gray-600 max-w-3xl mx-auto leading-relaxed">
        Describe your tool in one line.
      </p>
    </div>
    
    <!-- 工具区 -->
    <div class="bg-white rounded-2xl shadow-lg border border-gray-200 p-8">
      
      <!-- 输入框 -->
      <textarea 
        class="w-full h-[200px] lg:h-[300px] p-6 rounded-2xl border border-gray-200 
          bg-gray-50 focus:bg-white focus:border-gray-300 focus:ring-0 
          resize-none text-base placeholder:text-gray-400 transition-all"
        placeholder="Enter your text..."
      ></textarea>
      
      <!-- 快捷标签 (可选) -->
      <div class="flex flex-wrap gap-2 mt-6">
        <button class="px-4 py-2 bg-gray-50 hover:bg-gray-100 rounded-full text-xs font-medium">
          Option 1
        </button>
        <button class="px-4 py-2 bg-gray-50 hover:bg-gray-100 rounded-full text-xs font-medium">
          Option 2
        </button>
      </div>
      
      <!-- 生成按钮 -->
      <div class="mt-8">
        <button class="w-full sm:w-auto px-12 py-4 bg-gradient-to-r from-orange-400 to-orange-500 
          hover:from-orange-500 hover:to-orange-600 text-white rounded-full 
          font-semibold shadow-lg hover:shadow-xl transition-all transform hover:scale-105">
          ✨ Generate
        </button>
      </div>
      
    </div>
    
  </div>
</section>
```

**关键参数：**
- padding-top: `pt-20` (80px)
- 输入框高度: `h-[200px] lg:h-[300px]`
- 输入框背景: `bg-gray-50`
- 容器圆角: `rounded-2xl`
- 容器阴影: `shadow-lg`
- 按钮圆角: `rounded-full`
